Without a doubt, computing has changed the world. Computing has impacted the way we work, play, or even interact with others. Teenagers send Snapchats or they Venmo their friends. Despite its pervasiveness, the revolution had humble beginnings. It all began as the dream of a few scientists willing to take on risks for the vision they pursued.
